# Letter of Recruitment

## About the Research

### Title

Where to Look? Attention Guidance with Visual-Contextual Highlighting

### Description

When watching learning videos that use slides it is often **difficult to locate** where to focus on the slide at each point. Our research explores visual and contextual highlights on existing slide-videos to enhance accessibility for low vision (LV) users.

### The Study

We are looking for **low vision participants** to join our on-going user study. In this study participants will answer simple demographic questions, perform a dedicated task, and have a short semi-structured interview. The task includes watching a learning video using slides and answering a short multiple choice quiz.

#### Eligibility
- Low vision / partially sighted individuals
- Age 18 or older
- English; intermediate level or higher

#### Duration
The study will take approximately **1.5 hours** to complete.

#### Location
The study will be conducted remotely via Zoom.

#### Compensation
**30$ USD** Amazon gift card
